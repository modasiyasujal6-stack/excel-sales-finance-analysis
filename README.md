## Key Insights

- Revenue grew significantly from 2019 to 2021.
- Gross Margin % declined over time, indicating rising cost pressure.
- Some markets consistently missed sales targets.
- Customer concentration risk identified in top-performing accounts.
- Seasonal patterns visible in monthly sales performance.
